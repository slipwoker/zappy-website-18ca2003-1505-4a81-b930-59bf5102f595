Cannabis Cultivation & Agronomy

02 Manufacturing

Cannabis Manufacturing & Extraction

From crude extraction to finished consumer products, this program covers every stage of cannabis manufacturing with precision. Students gain command of solvent and solventless extraction techniques, refinement processes, and product formulation — all grounded in Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) standards and industry-grade quality control frameworks.

  • Execute CO₂, ethanol, hydrocarbon, and rosin extraction methods with laboratory-grade accuracy and safety protocols.
  • Formulate concentrates, edibles, tinctures, and topicals in compliance with state packaging, labeling, and dosage regulations.
  • Implement batch tracking, SOPs, and quality assurance testing workflows aligned with current GMP and lean manufacturing principles.
03 Medicine

Cannabis Medicine & Patient Care

One of the most rigorous medical cannabis courses available, this program bridges clinical pharmacology with patient-centered practice. Designed for healthcare professionals, patient advocates, and dispensary staff, it covers the endocannabinoid system, cannabinoid therapeutics, condition-specific protocols, and the ethical dimensions of cannabis-based patient care.

  • Analyze the endocannabinoid system and the pharmacokinetics of THC, CBD, and minor cannabinoids to guide evidence-based recommendations.
  • Develop condition-specific cannabis therapy protocols for pain management, neurological conditions, mental health, and palliative care.
  • Navigate drug interactions, contraindications, dosing strategies, and informed-consent practices across diverse patient populations.
04 Retail Compliance

Retail Operations & Regulatory Compliance

The cannabis retail environment demands a precise command of compliance, customer service, and operational management. This program prepares dispensary owners, managers, and frontline staff to operate with confidence — covering state licensing frameworks, seed-to-sale tracking, inventory control, responsible sales practices, and the legal obligations that govern every transaction.

  • Navigate state and municipal licensing requirements, application processes, and ongoing regulatory reporting obligations for cannabis retailers.
  • Implement seed-to-sale tracking systems, inventory auditing procedures, and loss-prevention strategies that withstand regulatory inspection.
  • Train staff in responsible vendor practices, ID verification, purchase limits, and ethical consultative sales to ensure compliant customer interactions.
